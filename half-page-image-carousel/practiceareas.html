Corporate & Commercial Law

Our corporate work includes advising on mergers and acquisitions startups, joint ventures and regulatory advice. We also have the capability to handle transactional, intellectual property and regulatory advisory work.

Corporate Services include:- • Mergers, Acquisitions and Disposals • General corporate, securities and business advice • Establishing a business in Uganda • Corporate structuring, and regulatory advice and compliance Commercial Services include:- • Leasing and Finance Agreements • Agency, Distribution and Licensing Agreements • Service Contracts, Consultancy Agreements and related Employment matters • Copyrights, Trademarks and other Intellectual Property matters

Litigation

Banking Litigation and Enforcement of Security • Arbitrations in Building & Engineering Construction • Negligence and other tort claims • Commercial Debt Recovery • Enforcing foreign judgments • Commercial and Contractual Litigation • Intellectual Property Litigation • Insurance Law • Employment and Labour Law • Construction Litigation
