Background

SWAN Legal is a young, vibrant and fresh firm on the Uganda Legal Market. It was borne from a merger of two legal consultancies; WALS Consultancy founded in 2008 and SNW Legal Consultants founded in 2011. The firm is a partnership of legal professionals with over 20 years of combined legal experience in general and specialized areas of law from government, corporate, donor, business, non-profit and private sectors.

In offering our legal services, we strive to apply principles of project management and other best practices applicable to professions of similar natures that are yet to be fully appreciated by the legal industry in Uganda. This helps us facilitate our client’s achievement of both their financial and business goals in term.

Our team of experts is keen to take on new, ground breaking, challenging and exciting areas of research and legal consultancy distinguishes its services as time efficient, reliable, credible and thorough.

The firm is medium-sized, comprised of six lawyers, two legal clerks and an administrative assistant. It’s distinctive competence is the ability to offer timely services with a personal touch. All the lawyers are always available at short notice to attend to clients’ matters without bureaucratic hassles.
