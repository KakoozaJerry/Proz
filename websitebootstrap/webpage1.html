About Us

The A.R.M.S. Project

The strategy of becoming the best at anything is making it about something you love, Evaluate your work and effectiveness, seeking support to improve and most importantly commitment. These are the qualities that every team member must have. In other words, love something and make it F'N awesome.
What does A.R.M.S. stand for?
Aside from the fact that A.R.M.S. actually stands for Academic Register Management Services, We stand for fun, honesty, truth, and above all, commitment. We believe in the due diligence required for a group of web developers such as ourselves to come together and find meaning in the creative designs and complex skeletons that bring web applications to life.
Who are part of A.R.M.S. Project?
Youth, enthusiastic about programming and web development. Basically, we are a team of people who love to code.
How can I join A.R.M.S. Project?
Easy. simply look out for the internship positions that are open every year and sign up. Be part of something big.
